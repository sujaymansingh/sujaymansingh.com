Note from the groom
I'm not very good at writing poems (or prose, or things that aren't computer code:)). However, given that it is for Shaina, I will make an extra special effort!
I still remember right at the beginning when she asked me whether I could speak Hindi: I told her I would on one condition. Before I could go further, she correctly guessed that the condition would be that she wouldn't laugh (and most of the time, she's managed to avoid laughing at my broken Hindi!). But more than that, it struck me how she just knew the right thing to say to me, whether it is some fun idea, or something supportive, or just something cute. And to this day, it's always been like that. Being around her, or sometimes just thinking about her, is enough to cheer me up no matter what.
She constantly says that I'm really nice to look out for her or similar things, but it's just feels natural for me to do so. All I want is to take care of Shaina and keep her happy. If I can make her even half as as happy as she makes me, then I'll consider my job done :)
Most of all when people ask me if I'm nervous about the wedding I realise that I'm nervous about many things (e.g. the venues for the event or sorting out the house here or dealing with Shaina's visa), but the one thing that never worries me is the idea of being with Shaina! Being with Shaina is just the perfect combination of exhilaration and comfort, and that's something I wouldn't change for the world!
I'm really looking forward to sharing my life with Shaina. I'm so incredibly lucky to have found someone as special as her and I know that a life with her will be a life lived to the fullest and most joyous :)
p.s. She's so kind and thoughtful that she's volunteered to take all of the ironing shifts in the house!
